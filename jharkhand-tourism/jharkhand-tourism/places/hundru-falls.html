About Hundru Falls

Hundru Falls is one of the most spectacular waterfalls in Jharkhand and the 34th highest waterfall in India. Located about 45 km from Ranchi, the Subarnarekha River plunges 98 meters (322 feet) down a rocky cliff, creating a mesmerizing cascade surrounded by dense tropical forest.

The waterfall is at its most magnificent during the monsoon season (July-September) when the river is in full flow. The thundering sound of water and the mist rising from the pool below create an unforgettable experience. During winter, the waterfall offers a more serene and picturesque setting, perfect for photography.

Visitors need to descend approximately 750 steps to reach the base of the falls, where a natural pool invites visitors for a refreshing dip. The surrounding area is rich in biodiversity with various plant species and birdlife. It's a popular spot for day picnics and weekend getaways from Ranchi.
