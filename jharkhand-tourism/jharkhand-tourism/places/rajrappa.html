About Rajrappa Temple

Rajrappa is one of the most revered pilgrimage sites in Jharkhand, located at the picturesque confluence of the Damodar and Bhairavi rivers in Ramgarh district. The temple is dedicated to Goddess Chinnamasta, one of the ten Mahavidyas in Hindu Shakti tradition.

The temple attracts thousands of devotees throughout the year, especially during Navratri and other auspicious occasions. The scenic beauty of the river confluence, surrounded by rocky terrain and lush greenery, makes it a unique blend of spirituality and natural wonder.

According to local legends, the temple has been a site of worship for centuries, and the self-manifested idol of Goddess Chinnamasta is believed to possess extraordinary spiritual power. The area around the temple is also popular for its natural beauty and is a favorite spot for picnics.
