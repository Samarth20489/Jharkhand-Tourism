Why Visit

Why Jharkhand?

🌊

Natural Waterfalls

Over 20 stunning waterfalls including Hundru, Dassam, Jonha and Lodh — the highest in the state at 143 meters.

🥁

Rich Tribal Culture

Home to 32 tribal communities with unique festivals, Paitkar paintings, Sohrai art, and vibrant folk music traditions.

🐘

Wildlife & Nature

Betla National Park shelters tigers, wild elephants and leopards across 1,135 sq km of pristine Palamau forests.
